Predicting Corporate Default based on Financial ratios

This project aims at predicting whether a company will default based on its fundamental financial information and historical financial ratios and default events of public companies over the 1995-2004 period.

Models are developed using Linear Regression and Gradient Boosting techniques.

Retail Mortgage Default Prediction & Loan-To-Value

This project examines data from sample accepted mortgage applications during 2009-2014, verifies data quality and implements necessary modifications for modeling purposes.

It also estimates the potential impact of LTV on the probability of default.
